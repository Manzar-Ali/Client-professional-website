About Luis del Rosal

Grew up in Southern California where he went to school in San Diego County, California.

He has worked, from a very young age, in the hotel industry. At the present time, his major hobby is writing children's books full of teachings an acceptance related issues for the younger ones in the family.

Luis del Rosal was born on March 16th 1973, a day before Saint Patrick's Day.
He enjoys traveling, listening to contemporary Rock and soft pop music. He also adores nature and animals. He now has a shih-tzu dog that he loves. Luis del Rosal has two siblings, an older sister and an older brother. He’s been currently living in Spain for over twenty years and he speaks Spanish and English fluently, also working on his French and Italian skills

This novel author enjoys writing stories for the little ones as it makes him feel productive and entertained. The main character from his first children's book is named Alberto and he is continuing to write new, fun and exciting adventures about this character. Alberto has faithful friends and faces daily life struggles and happiness. Luis del Rosal has had a a lot of struggles throughout his childhood.

Now he is trying to enjoy life to the fullest. Back in the USA, he studied journalism in California. He has had computer and internet skills since the beginning of that era in the 1990s.

Luis del Rosal is very extroverted with just about anyone he meets, he loves talking to people and having long conversations. Luis del Rosal is able to carry on a conversation basically about anything since he has read so much throughout his lifetime. He is young at heart, he does not look or act his age at all and his friends know him by “Luis, the big kid”. His ability to make new friends and start socializing is great, especially when he travels abroad. Luis del Rosal will soon start working on another children's book with a different character and a different perspective for the little ones to learn from. He has been a part of the city choir and a volunteer at the Red Cross in his younger years and is very empathic. Luis del Rosal loves helping out as much as possible. Kind-hearted, loving and with a temperament, Luis will never turn anyone down when it comes to having an interesting talk. Luis del Rosal is the perfect person to meet when wanting to share a different point of view.
